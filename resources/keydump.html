Dumping title keys with tik2sd

Title keys are needed to download base games for UWUVCI.
After purchasing a base from the eShop you can use tik2sd to obtain all title keys from your console.

Instructions:

  1. Insert your Wii U's SD Card into your Computer
  2. Download the tik2sd.elf from the latest tik2sd release
  3. Copy the tik2sd.elf to the /wiiu/apps folder on your SD Card
  4. Insert your SD Card back into your Wii U
  5. Launch the Homebrew Launcher, find the tik2sd.elf and open it
  6. Press "A" to dump your console tickets

    After the process is done you will be back in the Wii U menu

  7. Power off your console and insert the SD Card back into your PC
  8. Open the keys.txt file in the tik2sd folder on your SD Card with a text editor
  9. There should be several lines in that file with the title keys of your installed titles

Those lines will look something like this: 


Title ID         Title Key                       Title Path
0005000000000000 fffffffffffffffffffffffffffffff (0000/00000000.tik@0x0)

Search for the matching title id of your game and copy the title key into UWUVCI. You can search for it by going here.

Note that title keys are copyrighted and are not allowed to be shared!
